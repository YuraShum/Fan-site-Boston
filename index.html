Іме Удока

Udok is an American professional basketball coach who is the head coach of the NBA's Boston Celtics. Previously, Udoka worked as an assistant coach for the San Antonio Spurs, Philadelphia 76ers, and Brooklyn Nets. Udoka was a player of the Nigerian national team. At the 2006 World Cup, Udoka led Nigeria in scoring, assists and steals. Udoka also played for Nigeria at the 2005 and 2011 African (FIBA) Championships, winning the bronze medal in both tournaments.
