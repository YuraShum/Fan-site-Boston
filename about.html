Bill Russell

Russell is considered one of the best players in NBA history. Thanks to his height (2.08 meters), he blocked opponents' shots well, which was one of the main reasons for the success of the Boston Celtics. Russell also inspired the team's players to play defense. In addition, Russell played well on rebounds. He was the NBA's rebounding leader 4 times and ranks second all-time in rebounding and rebounding average per game. Also, Russell is one of two players in the history of the league (the second is Wilt Chamberlain) who made 50 rebounds in a game. Although he was never the Celtics' leading scorer, he scored 14,522 in his career with impressive assists to his teammates.

Jason Tatum

Jayson Christopher Tatum (born March 3, 1998 in St. Louis, Missouri, USA) is an American professional basketball player who plays for the Boston Celtics of the National Basketball Association. He plays as a light forward. Played for the Duke team in college basketball. Was selected in the 2017 NBA draft in the first round with the third overall pick.
